Siberian Husky

Information

The Siberian Husky is a breed of dog available in Nintendogs and its 3DS sequel. The Siberian Husky is a large breed of working dog that originated in Russia. An active, energetic and resilient breed whose ancestors came from the wolf, it was imported into Alaska during the Gold Rush and quickly spread from there into the United States & Canada as a popular sled dog.

Storlek och utseende

Nintendogs offers three different color variations for the husky: Black ("open face"), Dark Gray (with goggle patterns above the eyes), and Silver. In Nintendogs + Cats, the Siberian Husky comes in Black & White (shades from black to gray), Brown & White, and Agouti & White (from very pale brown and white to dark brown and white). If the player chooses "Surprise Me!" they may find: a "dirty face" agouti Husky (fully brown with a dark muzzle), a silver Husky or a pure white Husky.
